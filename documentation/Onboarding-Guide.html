BetSure has three primary user roles, each with specific responsibilities and access levels. Find your role below to understand what you'll be doing:

üéß Agent Role

Primary Responsibility: Making outbound calls to leads, following pitch scripts, and converting prospects into customers.

What You'll Do Daily:

  • Make outbound calls using the built-in softphone

  • Leads Tab: See all your assigned leads
  • Live Pitch Script: Follow real-time script guidance during calls

Getting Started as an Agent:

  1. Login to your dashboard
  2. Review your daily targets and current performance
  3. Check your leads queue - these are contacts assigned to you
  4. Test your headset and audio settings
  5. Start calling: Click "Call Next Lead" or select a specific lead
  6. Follow the AI script that appears during the call
  7. Complete post-call notes after each call (outcome, notes, next action)
  8. Monitor your performance throughout the day

üí° Key Concepts to Understand

1. Lead Lifecycle

Leads move through several stages in the system:

  1. New: Freshly imported, not yet contacted
  2. Assigned: Assigned to a specific agent
  3. In Progress: Agent has attempted contact
  4. Qualified: Lead shows interest and meets criteria
  5. Converted: Successful sale/conversion
  6. Lost: Not interested or unreachable

2. Call Status Types

  • Completed: Call connected and conversation took place
  • Failed: Call didn't connect (busy, no answer, wrong number)
  • No Answer: Phone rang but nobody picked up
  • Busy: Line was busy
  • Callback Requested: Lead asked to be called back later

3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

KPI Description Good Target
Dials per Day Total outbound calls attempted 80-100+
Connect Rate % of dials that reach a person 40-50%
Qualification Rate % of connects that become qualified 30-40%
Conversion Rate % of qualified leads that convert 15-25%
Average Handle Time Average call duration 3-5 minutes

4. AI Features

The platform uses AI to enhance performance:

  • Dynamic Scripts: AI generates contextual pitch scripts based on lead data
  • Sentiment Analysis: Real-time detection of customer mood during calls
  • Performance Insights: AI analyzes patterns to suggest improvements
  • Automated Reports: AI-generated insights from call data

üîç Common Issues & Solutions

Login Problems

Issue: Can't log in

  1. Verify you're using the correct URL
  2. Check your username/email and password (case-sensitive)
  3. Clear browser cache and cookies
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Contact your administrator to reset password if needed

Call Quality Issues

Issue: Poor audio quality or dropped calls

  1. Check your internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
  2. Test your headset/microphone
  3. Close unnecessary browser tabs
  4. Restart your browser
  5. Contact IT if issues persist

Missing Leads

Issue: No leads showing in queue

  1. Check if leads have been assigned to you (Agents)
  2. Verify your status is set to "Online"
  3. Refresh the page
  4. Contact your manager or administrator
üìû Technical Support: If you encounter persistent issues, contact your IT department or system administrator with a description of the problem, screenshots, and any error messages.
